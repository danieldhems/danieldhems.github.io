  • F1 Connectivity Innovation Prize

    Overview

    Website for F1 Connectivity Innovation Prize competition run by Tata Communications. Competition is intended to bring about innovation in the sport by calling on members of the public to propose new ways of delivering the crucial race data that Tata provides. The competition comprises of three unique challenges that address a specific technical problem. Participants then have a specific time-frame in which to find and submit a creative solution.

    Submission is made via the entry page on the website, and requires the participant's basic information, description of their solution and at least one supporting image or document that illustrates their solution.

    The competition has had a strong response overall, and has received a great variety of high-calibre entries.

    At the time of this writing, the winners for the first of the three challenges has been publicly announced, and the second challenge will soon be launched.
